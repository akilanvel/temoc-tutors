Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my appointments?

+

You can cancel appointments at no charge if it is done more than 24 hours before the appointment. Otherwise, there won't be a refund.

How do I reset my password?

+

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password. If you have any issues, please contact customer support.

What does the search bar do in the Tutors page?

+

It searches tutors by name.
